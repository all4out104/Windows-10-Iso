Create a Bootable Pen Drive in MBR

Requirements

  • A USB pen drive with at least 4GB of storage
  • An ISO file of the operating system you want to install
  • Rufus or any other bootable USB creation tool

Steps to Create a Bootable Pen Drive

  1. Insert your USB pen drive into your computer.
  2. Download and open Rufus.
  3. Select your USB pen drive from the 'Device' dropdown.
  4. Click on the 'Select' button and choose your ISO file.
  5. Under 'Partition scheme', select 'MBR'.
  6. Click 'Start' to begin the process.
  7. Wait for the process to complete, and safely eject your USB pen drive.
